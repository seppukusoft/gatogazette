The 136th Senate Candidates Playlist

Gato March 1, 2025

As part of the recent Senate Candidate Survey, we asked respondents to name their current favorite song. We took all of the answers available and put them into a playlist so everyone can enjoy a variety of new music that your potential new senators enjoy!

We'd love to hear feedback on this idea and whether or not you'd like this to be a permanent fixture of the survey.
